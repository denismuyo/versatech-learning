Adobe photoshop Learning Curriculum Overview

Adobe Photoshop is a powerful graphic design tool used for creating and editing images, designing posters, social media graphics, and much more. This curriculum focuses on hands-on skills to help learners become proficient in poster design using Photoshop.

Course Modules
Introduction to Photoshop – Understanding the interface, tools, and basic functions
Working with Layers – Managing layers, blending modes, and layer styles
Typography & Text Effects – Creating visually appealing text elements
Image Editing & Manipulation – Adjusting brightness, contrast, and applying filters
Designing a Poster – Combining elements to create professional posters
Final Project – Practical application of learned skills by designing a creative poster

By completing this course, learners will gain hands-on experience in Photoshop, enhancing their design skills for personal and professional use.
